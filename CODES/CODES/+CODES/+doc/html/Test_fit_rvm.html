Simple example

Define a simple sinusoidal problem:

f=@(x)sin(x)./x;
x=CODES.sampling.cvt(10,1,'lb',-10,'ub',10);
y=f(x);

Build and plot a Kriging without CI:

rvm=CODES.fit.rvm(x,y,'scale','none');

figure('Position',[200 200 500 500])
plot(linspace(-10,10,100)',f(linspace(-10,10,100)'),'ro--')
hold on
rvm.plot('prev_leg',{'true'})
